Parangkusumo Beach

Parangkusumo is a natural beach who located in Yogyakarta, Yogyakarta.

It is about 46 km from city of Yogyakarta. You can go to there by Motorcycle or car.

It is a beautyful beach with the big waves so be carefull for the high wave if you want to

swim in this beach. FYI... I was enjoying the light of sun in this beach for 3 hour.
